# Rules for Bourbon Trail "Game"
## Team selection system
On your profile, select whether to package with friends and if you have a preference of team Lin or Ditty preference (or neither). Teams will be manually selected by captains based on everyone’s preference. The players essentially choose their own feat. This will all be revealed at the Alumni Retreat. 

## Committees
Everyone not a captain must be in a committee. These committees are designed such that everyone has a contributing role in the overall trip. The numbers represent the size of each committee:
* Lead (2) - Each team has one lead member to make group decisions and represent their teams for the games.
* Food (3) - Decide where to eat lunch/dinners and how to split the group, if necessary. 
* Instagram/pics (3) - Keep the shared instagram account active and take pics/videos for the YouTube video Andrew plans to make afterwards.  
* Venmo logging (2) - Keep receipts for all group purchases and log them in the venmo group, whether it was on their card or not. 
* Uber/lyft (3) - Order rideshare. For the group of 18, we should only need three UberXL’s at most, as each can hold up to 6 people. 
* Navigation/Crowd Control (3) - Guide the group to our destinations. Also responsible for gathering everyone to prepare for transportation. 

## Point system
Each team earns points through events before and during the trip. 

## Awards
There will be post-season awards announced after the trip. Note that this doesn’t count towards team total points. These will all be voted on after the trip. Here are the awards:
* MVP - most enhanced the overall trip throguh contribution, entertainment, and performance in competitions. 
* Bus MVP - most enhanced the bus experience to recognize the person who turns transportation time into part of the trip. 
* Sixth Man of the Year - Best non-captain, non-council contributor. The unsung hero. 
* Clutch player of the year - consistently delivered in high-pressure moments. When the lights were brightest, who showed up?
* All-Bourbon First Team
* Best dressed
* Biggest liability
